What we are doing

Our activities

We cares for approximately 200 visually impaired girls.

Fundraising

Some students endeavour to pursue vocational courses available in local institutes where they can learn physiotherapy, beauty care, use of Braille computers and to become telephone operators.

Selling Products

To train the girls in vocations that help them achieve self- reliance. To develop their musical talents To teach cooking & home – management. To rehabilitate them in society.

Housing

They also provide hostel facility for blind girls. The school has invested in a full fledged laboratory with models and charts used to explain principles of science & the environment.

Education

It offers lodging and boarding and in-house facilities in Braille education up to Class VII. For further studies up to Board level and for Bachelor and Masters degrees, girls attend local schools and colleges meant for sighted students.

Charity

Overnight trips and full day picnics are a regular feature. The children enjoy visits to the circus, military parades, parks and neighbouring temples.
