About Us

Neet-Vasant means Spring Forever; the Company is founded by Mr. Darshan Soni in August-2017 with an aim to serve Renewables & Conventional energy sectors.

Mr. Soni is a Mechanical Engineer from Saurashtra University (2005-09), pursued PGDM in Petroleum Management from India’s premium petroleum university PDPU (2013-15), Certifications in Operations Management from IIM-Bangalore (2016) & Projects Management from University of Adelaide (2016).

Mr. Soni possesses 8.5+ years of experience in downstream Oil & Gas Industry & prior founding Neet-Vasant he has been working with India’s largest natural gas distribution company & a Govt. of Gujarat Undertaking for 7.5+ years.

QUALITY POLICY

Neet-Vasant Enterprise firmly believes in quality products & services shall be delivered.
